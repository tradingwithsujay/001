My Trading Philosophy

"I don’t believe trading is about being right. I believe it’s about managing risk when you’re wrong."

I started trading like most people — searching for certainty, patterns, and high-probability setups. Over time, I realised the market doesn’t reward prediction it rewards repetition. What works is a minimal, rule-based approach focused on price behavior, risk management, consistency and psychological control.
